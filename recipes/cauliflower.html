Cauliflower with Potatoes

Description

This dish goes great with lentils and rice or flat bread. This recipe is ayurvedic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons clarified butter (ghee)
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 4 medium onions, chopped
  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 medium tomato, quartered
  • 1 medium cauliflower, cut into small florets
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine 1/2 cup water, turmeric, coriander, and both gingers. Stir and set aside.
  2. In a frying pan, heat the clarified butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the garlic and onions and cook until light brown.
  4. Stir in the spice mixture.
  5. Add the potatoes and the tomato pieces and cook until they begin to soften.
  6. Add the cauliflower pieces, water and salt. Cook until soft, about 15 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle on the black pepper and cinnamon, then serve.
